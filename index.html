About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic people with common interest in aeromodelling. The team is a splendid blend of talent, creativity, experience and innovation. We are the Pioneers in aero modelling, an official technical team of NIT Jamshedpur known for manufacturing radio controlled planes. through our sheer experience and hardwork, we have always brought laurels to the institute.

Vision

Team Phoenix aims at promoting awareness regarding the aviation sector and develop real time solutions to ongoing issues in this field in the country. The team members share their Engineering instincts to study various parameters that effect performance and generate a practical layout which can be executed effectively.

What we do?

We build RC planes under different design constraints and compete in SAE India and international.

CAD Designing

CATIA V5 R20 is used as a designing tool to model our ideas. The location of CG and weight of the aircraft are analysed by assigning different material properties for the concerned part bodies.

Analysis

ANSYS R19.2 is used as an analysis tool. Attachments between various interfaces are analysed and developed using Static and Dynamic structural analysis. Modal analysis is done to examine aeroelasticity.Further tools like XFLR5 and MATLAB are used to calculate drag and efficiency of the model.

Fabrication

Team Phoenix very well knows that structure holds a high ground in building anything that thrives. Fabrication is that part of the process where we bring together our designs and materials with very careful considerations to get the final product. The team works on the fabrication process with the primary objective in the mind to get a plane that is efficient, lightweight yet robust, pragmatic, and simultaneously cater to all the rules of the competition. Fabrication involves extensive research over the material selection, working on wing and tail structure, and a set of manufacturing techniques.
