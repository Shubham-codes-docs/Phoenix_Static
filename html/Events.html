QUEST the SKY

Team Phoenix, the official aerodynamic team of NIT Jamshedpur, presents its first event of the year “Quest the Sky”. It will give you an insight of the history of planes, different parts of a plane and the technical aspects of a plane. This is a golden opportunity for all aerodynamic enthusiasts to showcase their passion in the world of aviation.
There are three parts of the event:

  1. Soaring into the blue: The Wright Brothers' quiz
  2. Do you speak ‘plane’? : The Crossword
  3. An aerodynamic pursuit: The hunt for treasure

The world of aerodynamics is alluring and thrilling. So, fasten your seat belts and let your dreams take flight. If you’ve ever wanted to build a plane on your own, Team Phoenix is where your quest ends. Show your enthusiasm and interest by participating in the events!

Ever wondered how the two brothers who made the world's first successfully operated motor airplane start? It was 230 years ago when the daredevil flyers launched themselves into the air. To commemorate the conquerors of the sky, this event will take you back in time to where it all started. FLY RIGHT IN!
